## Structure

Each meetup session file in `docs/meetup/` contains:

* Session metadata (organization, date, duration, type, deliverable)
* Why This Session (context and importance)
* Agenda (detailed breakdown of topics)
* Related Content (links to learning sessions and master plan)

---

**Note**: Meetup sessions are based on the learning sessions but include meetup-specific details such as delivery dates, agenda for live presentation, and meetup organization information.
