### `01_reasoning-foundations/` - SHARED
**For**: All roles  
**What**: How to think about problems, ask questions, identify constraints  
**When**: Study first, before everything else

### `02_answer-structuring/` - SHARED
**For**: All roles  
**What**: How to structure your answers, control depth, communicate  
**When**: Study after foundations, use in all interviews

### `03_tradeoff-articulation/` - SHARED + ROLE-SPECIFIC
**For**: All roles (some files are role-specific)  
**What**: How to reason about trade-offs, frame risks  
**When**: Study after foundations, practice articulating trade-offs

### `04_role-perspectives/` - SHARED
**For**: All roles  
**What**: Understanding different role mindsets  
**When**: Read to understand how different roles think

### `05_evaluation-scenarios/` - MIXED
**For**: Varies by scenario  
**What**: Practice scenarios with context, ambiguities, solutions  
**When**: After studying foundations, practice for your role

### `06_role-preparation/` - ROLE-SPECIFIC
**For**: Your specific role  
**What**: Complete preparation guide for your role  
**When**: This is your main guide - follow it step by step
