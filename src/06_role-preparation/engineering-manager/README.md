# Engineering Manager Role Preparation

## Role Focus

**Engineering Managers** focus on:
- People management and team building
- Technical leadership and strategy
- DevOps and infrastructure
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Delivery and execution

## Interview Tips

- **Balance people and technical** - Both are important
- **Show leadership** - How you build and lead teams
- **Demonstrate technical depth** - You understand the work
- **Show delivery focus** - Results matter
- **Explain trade-offs** - Every decision has trade-offs

## Common Questions

- "How do you build high-performing teams?"
- "How do you handle underperformance?"
- "How do you make technical decisions?"
- "How do you balance technical debt and delivery?"
- "How do you work with product and other teams?"
